Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I wash my genitals?
It's generally recommended to wash your genital area daily, using mild soap and water. However, excessive washing or harsh soaps can disrupt the natural balance, so moderation is key.
Is it necessary to trim pubic hair for hygiene?
Trimming or shaving pubic hair is a personal preference. Keeping the area clean is more important than the hair itself. If you choose to trim, do it carefully to avoid skin irritation.
How can I prevent genital odor?
Regular washing, wearing breathable underwear, and changing into clean, dry clothes can help prevent genital odor. Avoiding tight-fitting clothing can also reduce sweat buildup.
How can I prevent jock itch and other fungal infections?
Keep the genital area clean and dry, especially after sweating. Wear breathable underwear, avoid tight clothing, and change out of wet clothes promptly.
When should I see a doctor about genital issues?
If you notice persistent itching, redness, swelling, unusual discharge, or any other changes in the genital area, it's essential to consult a healthcare professional for proper diagnosis and treatment.
